# Amazon — Leadership Principles Interview Framework

Every answer must map to a Leadership Principle (LP).

## How to Structure Answers
LP → STAR → LP reinforcement at the close.

## 16 Leadership Principles
1. Customer Obsession
2. Ownership
3. Invent and Simplify
4. Are Right, A Lot
5. Learn and Be Curious
6. Hire and Develop the Best
7. Insist on the Highest Standards
8. Think Big
9. Bias for Action
10. Frugality
11. Earn Trust
12. Dive Deep
13. Have Backbone; Disagree and Commit
14. Deliver Results
15. Strive to be Earth's Best Employer
16. Success and Scale Bring Broad Responsibility

## Interview Format
- Typically 5-6 interviews in a loop (virtual or onsite)
- Each interviewer assigned 2-3 LPs to evaluate
- "Tell me about a time when..." is the standard format
- Bar raiser present — one interviewer from outside the team

## Tips
- Prepare 2 stories per LP (they ask follow-ups)
- Always quantify results — Amazon is data-driven
- Interviewers score on specific LP rubrics
- STAR format is expected — don't deviate
- "What did YOU do?" — they want individual contribution
- Recent examples preferred (last 2-3 years)
- Failure stories are just as important as success stories
