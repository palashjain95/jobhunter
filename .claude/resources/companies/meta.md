# Meta — Product Execution + Leadership + Drive

## Interview Format
- Typically 4-5 interviews for PM roles
- Rounds: Product Sense, Execution, Leadership & Drive
- Fast-paced, metrics-heavy

## Product Execution
"How would you measure success for [feature]?"
"Metric X dropped 10% — what do you do?"

### Structure
Goal → Metrics → Diagnosis → Solution → Tradeoffs

### Tips
- Always define the North Star metric first
- Break metrics into a tree (DAU = new users + retained + resurrected - churned)
- For metric drops: external vs internal, segment by platform/geo/user type
- Always discuss tradeoffs — Meta values pragmatic thinking
- Think about counter-metrics (what could go wrong if this metric improves)

## Leadership & Drive
- "Move fast" culture — show speed and decisiveness
- "What's the hardest problem you've solved?"
- "Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with limited data"
- They want intensity, ownership, and bias toward action
- Failure stories should show what you learned and how you moved faster next time

## Product Sense
- "Design a product for [use case] within Meta's ecosystem"
- Think about how it connects to Meta's family of apps
- Consider network effects and social dynamics
- Monetization awareness (ads model) is a plus
