# Microsoft — Growth Mindset + Competency-Based

## Interview Format
- Typically 4-5 interviews for PM roles
- Mix of: behavioral, product design, analytical, leadership
- Often includes a presentation or case study round
- Competency-based with strong emphasis on Growth Mindset

## Growth Mindset (Core Cultural Value)
Satya Nadella's transformation of Microsoft centers on this:
- "Tell me about a time you changed your mind based on new information"
- "What's something you failed at and what did you learn?"
- "How do you stay curious in your domain?"
- They want learn-it-alls, not know-it-alls
- Show you embrace challenges, learn from criticism, find inspiration
  in others' success

## Competency Framework
Microsoft evaluates on specific competencies per level:
- **Customer Obsession** — "How do you ensure you're building what
  customers actually need?"
- **Drive for Results** — "Tell me about a time you delivered under
  tight constraints"
- **Collaboration** — "How do you work across teams with different
  priorities?" (critical — Microsoft is heavily matrixed)
- **Influencing for Impact** — "How do you get buy-in without authority?"
- **Judgment** — "Tell me about a tough call you made with limited data"

## Product Questions
- "How would you improve [Microsoft product]?"
- Think about the Microsoft ecosystem (Azure, M365, Teams, Copilot)
- Consider enterprise vs consumer dynamics
- AI/Copilot integration is top of mind — show you think about
  how AI changes the product landscape

## Tips
- Know the mission: "Empower every person and every organization on
  the planet to achieve more"
- Reference Satya's transformation — shows you understand the culture shift
- Microsoft values inclusive design and accessibility deeply
- Don't trash competitors — they partner with many of them
- Show you can navigate a large, matrixed org
- Mention growth mindset naturally, don't force it
