# Apple — Design + Cross-Functional + Secrecy

## Interview Format
- Highly secretive about process and role details
- Multiple rounds, often including a presentation
- Heavy emphasis on collaboration and design thinking

## What They Test

### Design Taste
- "Why is this product designed this way?"
- "What would you change about [Apple product]?"
- Show you think about the full user experience, not just features
- Attention to detail matters — they notice how you think about craft

### Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Apple is organized by function, not product
- Your stories must show working across engineering, design, marketing
- "Tell me about a time you had to align multiple teams"
- They value people who can navigate complex org structures

### Comfort with Ambiguity and Secrecy
- Don't expect them to tell you much about the role upfront
- Show you can work without full context
- Demonstrate discretion — they value people who don't overshare

## Tips
- Research their recent product launches and design philosophy
- Know their values: privacy, accessibility, environmental responsibility
- Don't criticize Apple products — suggest thoughtful improvements
- Show genuine passion for craft and user experience
- Be prepared to present or whiteboard
