# General / Unknown Company Interview Framework

Use this when the company doesn't have a well-known interview
framework, or when you can't find specific intel.

## How to Prepare

1. Search for "[company] interview process" on Glassdoor and Blind
2. Check if they publish values or principles on their website
3. Look at their careers page for clues about culture
4. Ask the candidate: "Do you know what their interview format is?"
5. Default to standard behavioral (STAR) + role-specific technical

## Standard Behavioral Interview

### Common Question Categories
- **Leadership:** "Tell me about a time you led a team..."
- **Conflict:** "Describe a disagreement with a colleague..."
- **Failure:** "What's your biggest professional mistake?"
- **Achievement:** "What are you most proud of?"
- **Motivation:** "Why this role? Why this company? Why now?"

### STAR Format (always)
- **Situation:** Brief context (2-3 sentences)
- **Task:** Your specific responsibility
- **Action:** What YOU did (not the team)
- **Result:** Quantified outcome + what you learned

### Time Management
- Behavioral answers: 2 minutes max
- If they want more, they'll ask follow-ups
- Practice the clock — most candidates go too long

## Role-Specific Prep
- **PM roles:** Product sense, metrics, prioritization, stakeholder management
- **Engineering:** System design, coding, architecture decisions
- **Strategy:** Market analysis, competitive dynamics, business cases
- **Operations:** Process improvement, data analysis, cross-functional coordination
