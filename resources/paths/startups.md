# Startups / Growth-Stage Interview Framework

## Interview Format
- Less structured than big tech — often conversational
- Typically: founder/CEO chat, team interview, practical exercise
- Culture fit weighs as heavily as skills
- May include a take-home or trial project

## What They Test

### Founder Mindset
- 0→1 building experience — "Have you built something from scratch?"
- Wearing multiple hats — "Tell me about a time you did something
  outside your job description"
- Resourcefulness — "How would you solve X with limited budget?"
- Speed — "What's the fastest you've shipped something?"

### First 30/60/90 Days
Common question: "What would you do in your first 30 days?"
Structure:
- Days 1-30: Listen, learn, map the landscape, build relationships
- Days 31-60: Identify quick wins, propose a plan, start executing
- Days 61-90: Deliver first meaningful result, establish credibility

### Culture and Mission Alignment
- Show genuine enthusiasm for their specific mission
- "Why this startup and not [big tech company]?"
- Know their product inside out — sign up, use it, have opinions
- Ask about their biggest challenge right now

## Questions to Ask
- What does success look like for this role in 6 months?
- What's the team structure and who would I work with?
- How are decisions made? (consensus vs top-down)
- What's your runway and growth trajectory?
- What's the biggest risk to the business right now?
